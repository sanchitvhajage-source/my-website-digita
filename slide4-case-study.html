The Problem

Traditional flood warning systems often suffer from delays and lack the granular, reliable data needed for residents to make informed safety decisions. This communication gap, highlighted by events like the Punjab floods, can lead to significant property damage and risk to human life.

The Solution

AquaSentry is a dedicated mobile-first platform designed to bridge this information gap. By aggregating data from official APIs and using a predictive Hybrid Dual-Model AI Architecture, it provides hyper-local, high-confidence flood warnings, minimizing cognitive load during a crisis.

My Role

I led the full end-to-end development and architectural design of the AquaSentry system. I designed the Hybrid Dual-Model Architecture and developed the complete native Android application in Java using Android Studio. My implementation focused on concurrency management for stable data fetching and engineering low-level network resilience for older devices.
