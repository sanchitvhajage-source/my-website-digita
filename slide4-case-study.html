The Problem

Traditional flood warning systems often suffer from delays and lack the granular, real-time data needed for residents to make informed safety decisions. This communication gap can lead to significant property damage and risk to human life when evacuation routes become unclear or warnings arrive too late.

The Solution

The AquaSentry App is a mobile-first platform designed to bridge this information gap. By aggregating data from official sensors and user-submitted reports, it provides hyper-local, real-time flood warnings. Its core features include an interactive safety map showing affected areas and clear evacuation routes, push notifications for immediate alerts, and a community reporting system to empower users with the latest on-the-ground information.
