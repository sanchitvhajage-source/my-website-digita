The Problem

Traditional flood warning systems often suffer from delays and lack the granular, real-time data needed for residents to make informed safety decisions. This communication gap can lead to significant property damage and risk to human life.

The Solution

AquaSentry is a mobile-first platform designed to bridge this information gap. By aggregating data from official sensors and using predictive AI, it provides hyper-local, real-time flood warnings. Its core features include an interactive safety map, push notifications, and a community reporting system.
